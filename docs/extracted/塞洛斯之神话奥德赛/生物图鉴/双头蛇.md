# 双头蛇

双头蛇 Amphisbaena 双头蛇的身体两端各生着一个头颅，每个头颅都长有毒牙。为了移动，一只双头蛇会用一个头抓紧另外一个头的脖子，形成一个呼啦圈在地上滚动。

The amphisbaena has a head at either end of its serpentine body, and each head sports venomous fangs. To move, an amphisbaena uses one mouth to grip the neck of its other head, forming a hoop that rolls over the ground.
